# Docty Clinics Patient App

## Overview

Docty Clinics is a rapidly growing neighbourhood healthcare network focused on making quality healthcare accessible, affordable, and available 24/7. This patient app provides a digital front door to their integrated healthcare services — enabling patients to book appointments, order pharmacy, schedule lab tests, explore health packages, find clinic locations, and subscribe to membership plans.

## Roles

### Patient
The primary user — individuals and families seeking convenient access to healthcare services.
- Browse available services and clinic locations
- Book doctor appointments across specialties
- Order pharmacy medications for home delivery
- Schedule lab tests and diagnostics
- Purchase health checkup packages
- Subscribe to Docty Total Care membership plans
- Access emergency/quick care information

## Key Scenarios

1. **Book a Doctor Appointment**: Patient selects service type, preferred clinic location, date/time, and confirms booking
2. **Explore Health Packages**: Compare seasonal health checkup packages (Monsoon, Women's, Men's, Child, Senior) with pricing
3. **Find Nearest Clinic**: View all 4 Hyderabad locations (Lanco Hills, Manikonda, Nallagandla, Sainikpuri) with hours and contact
4. **Subscribe to Membership**: Explore Docty Total Care benefits (₹999/year) including unlimited consultations and discounts
5. **Order Pharmacy**: Browse and order medicines for home delivery
6. **Book Lab Test**: Schedule diagnostic tests with quick report turnaround

## Design Direction

**Aesthetic**: Clean, trustworthy, and approachable healthcare experience. Modern but warm — not sterile or clinical.

**Brand Colors**:
- **Vibrant Red (#FE065C)**: Primary CTAs, emergency indicators, key accents
- **Vibrant Blue (#0BB8FC)**: Secondary elements, links, informational highlights
- **White**: Clean backgrounds with generous breathing room

**Typography**: Rounded, friendly sans-serif for headings conveying approachability. Clean body text for readability.

**Visual Identity**: The Docty logo features a distinctive medical cross formed by rounded pill shapes in red and blue. This playful yet professional mark reinforces the brand's neighbourhood clinic positioning.

**Tone**: Welcoming, reassuring, community-focused. "Care That's Close to Home."
